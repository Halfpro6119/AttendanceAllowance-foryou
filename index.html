What is Attendance Allowance?

DWP Attendance Allowance provides extra financial support for people over 66 whose health or daily activities are limited. Tax-free and not means-tested – your savings or income won't affect eligibility. Spend it on care, transport, heating, or whatever helps most.

Eligibility

  • Health or daily activities limited
  • Need care from another person
  • Needed care for 6+ months
  • State pension age or over (66+)
  • Not claiming PIP or DLA

Note: Not means-tested. You don't need to be receiving care to qualify.

2025 rates

  • Lower: £320.23/month
  • Higher: £478.40/month

Rates and rules are set by the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP). See official guidance on GOV.UK.

In Scotland: Pension Age Disability Payment (PADP).
